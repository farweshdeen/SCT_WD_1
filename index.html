About Us

Inspiring curiosity beyond the sky

Our Astronomy Community

VAAN Foundation is a community-driven initiative that brings astronomy and space science closer to everyday people. We believe the night sky should feel welcoming, exciting, and deeply human.

Through public observation sessions, workshops, quizzes, and outreach programs, we help students, families, and curious minds build a genuine connection with the universe.

VAAN Foundation Mission

Our mission is to make astronomy and space science accessible through simple explanations, live skywatching, educational content, and community events that encourage scientific thinking and wonder.

We organize telescope sessions, eclipse awareness programs, science talks, astronomy quizzes, and citizen-science style learning experiences that inspire people to look up and ask questions.
